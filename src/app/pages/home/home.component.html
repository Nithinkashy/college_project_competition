About the Competition

InnovateX is an annual inter-collegiate project competition that brings together the most innovative student projects from across the country.

This year's theme is "Sustainable Solutions for Tomorrow's Challenges". We're looking for projects that address real-world problems with creative, implementable solutions.

Whether you're working on a tech innovation, social initiative, business idea, or creative project, InnovateX provides the perfect platform to showcase your work to industry experts and potential investors.

Why Participate?

Connect with industry leaders, potential investors, and like-minded innovators.

Expert Mentorship
